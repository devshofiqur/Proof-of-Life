One Honest Conversation.

We're not experts. We're two best friends in our 40s who decided the most useful thing we could do was stop lying to each other — and start recording it.

Maya
Holloway
Host No. 01

Maya Holloway

Entrepreneur · Mother of Three · Recovering Perfectionist

I spent fifteen years building a business, a marriage, and a very convincing performance of having it all together. At 42, I finally admitted to my best friend that I had no idea what I was doing — and that it was one of the most liberating things I'd ever said.

I talk about entrepreneurship, the real cost of ambition, what it means to mother three kids while also desperately wanting to be a person, and the years I wasted trying to be the version of myself I thought everyone expected.

I'm here because the conversation that happens after the kids are in bed — the real one — is the one nobody's having publicly. Until now.

Host No. 02

Serena Cross

Creative Director · Bonus Mom · Chronic Overthinker

I've reinvented myself three times. Left a marriage that looked perfect. Fell in love again in my late 30s. Became a bonus mom to two kids who didn't ask for me, and somehow that became the most important thing I've ever done.

My thing is the identity stuff — who you are when the role you built yourself around disappears. I left a seventeen-year career to start over at 41. It was terrifying and completely the right call. I talk about that a lot.

I started this podcast because I needed the conversation. Turns out, so did everyone else.
